My Experience

My mom joined a trip travel for woman on January, 2019. My older sister and I also joined it because we never been to oversea.
It's was an exciting moments. We have to make our passport at Immigration Department of Malaysia.
We took a train from our town, Kampar, Perak, to Kuala Lumpur. We have to took a bus to go to Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KLIA).
Our first experience ride a flight was the best. Eventhough when the plane want to departure, it was kind of scary, but eventually we make it.
When we arrive at Ho Chi Minh City Airport, we were so excited. Traffic were congestion as the population of this city were compact.
